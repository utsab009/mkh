A Great Way to Avoid or Solve Problems

Before your meeting is scheduled to start, open Try A Mentor, sign-in and go to your Inbox (if you are the Mentee go to 'From Mentors' section of your inbox, if you are the Mentee go to 'From mentees' section of the inbox). You can type a message to your Mentor / Mentee to say you are about to connect, and they can reply in real time. This way you are telling the other person you are ready and waiting and if either of you run into problems, the other person can offer help.
