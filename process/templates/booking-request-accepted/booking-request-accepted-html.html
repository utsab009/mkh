A Great Way to Avoid or Solve Problems

Before your meeting is scheduled to start, open Try A Mentor, sign-in and go to your Inbox (From Mentors). You can type a message to your Mentor to say you are about to connect, and they can reply in real time. This way they can offer help if you run into any problems. If you are not sure how to get Skype set-up, remember your Mentor is here to help from this point forward, so again, through your Try A Mentor inbox, send them messages asking for help and combine the advice given with the many 'how to' videos you will find on the internet (just google 'how to set up and use Skype').

Need to Postpone or Cancel the Meeting

Remember you can cancel at no cost up to 48 hours before the meeting but within 48 hours you will be charged the full fee with one exception. The exception is, If you book the meeting within the 48 hours period (e.g. you book a meeting for the next day) you can get a refund if you cancel within 15 minutes of when you made the booking. You can cancel three times a year and will receive a full refund but from the fourth time onwards, Try A Mentor reserves the right to retain the Try A Mentor administration fee.
